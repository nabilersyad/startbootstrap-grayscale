Aguas Calientes

After 3 hours of walking along train tracks, we finally made to the tiny little town of Aguas Calientes. It's a base from which visitors stop at before making their ascend up the hill to Machu Picchu by either bus or hike. The sight of this tiny town was quite a relief after the monotonous walk.
